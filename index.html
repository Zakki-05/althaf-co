Preserving Ancient Wisdom for Modern Wellness

Since 1984, Althaf&co has been dedicated to the timeless power of nature's remedies. Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring the authentic benefits of Ayurvedic herbs to health-conscious individuals worldwide.

Myrobalan, known as Kadukkai in Tamil, has been revered in Ayurveda for centuries. We source our myrobalan from organic farms in the pristine regions of South India, where traditional farming methods ensure the highest quality and potency.

Every batch is carefully processed to preserve the natural compounds that make myrobalan a cornerstone of holistic wellness. From farm to package, we maintain strict quality controls to deliver nothing but the purest product to your doorstep.
